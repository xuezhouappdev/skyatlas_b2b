About Skyatlas

SkyAtlas International Inc. locates in Boston, Massachusetts. We are dedicated to provide experiential vacation packages for visitors all around the world.
Led by travel planners with over 20 years of experience, we boast one of a kind immersive travel experience for quality-seekers. We currently have a rich portfolio of featured itineraries for major tourist destinations in the U.S., especially on the east coast, along with summer camp options for teenagers.
SkyAtlas International has the most comprehensive knowledge to curate unique traveling experiences for all kinds of groups and individuals. We provide hand-selected hotels, themed tours and exclusive services to U.S. destinations, with 24/7 customer support.
Besides the themed travel packages, SkyAtlas International leverages its strong connections with the education and business world to provide customized trip planning and operations including event arrangement, higher education institution visits, Fortune 500 company trek and management meetings to fuel customers’ personal and business growth.
SkyAtlas International also boasts a professional market intelligence team to closely monitor the latest trend and consumer insights in the travel industry and analyze first-hand market data to support market penetration. We are willing to share such insights with other travel product providers via our consulting business.
